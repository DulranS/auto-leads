## 🎯 Core Features Explained

### 1. Advanced Analytics Engine ✅
**Problem It Solves**: "Am I actually making money?"

**Features**:
- Calculates actual ROI (Revenue - Cost / Cost)
- Shows conversion rate at each funnel stage
- Compares performance across email, WhatsApp, SMS, calls
- Identifies best-performing lead source
- Calculates Customer Lifetime Value

**Business Impact**: Know exactly where money goes, scale what works

### 2. Deal Pipeline Management ✅
**Problem It Solves**: "Will I hit my revenue target?"

**Features**:
- Assigns probability to each deal stage
- Calculates weighted pipeline value
- Forecasts 90-day revenue
- Detects stalled deals (14+ days in stage)
- Suggests specific next actions

**Business Impact**: Accurate forecasting, catch problems early

### 3. Predictive Analytics ✅
**Problem It Solves**: "When should I contact leads? Will they buy?"

**Features**:
- Closure probability per lead (0-100%)
- Best contact day/time with timezone awareness
- Churn risk scoring for existing customers
- Upsell opportunity detection
- Price sensitivity estimation

**Business Impact**: Contact at right time, prevent churn, close more deals

### 4. Smart Lead Assignment ✅
**Problem It Solves**: "Who should get this lead?"

**Features**:
- Capacity-based distribution (balanced workload)
- Territory-based routing
- Conversion-rate optimization (best reps get hot leads)
- Industry/specialization matching
- Automated recommendations

**Business Impact**: Higher close rates, fair workload, happier team

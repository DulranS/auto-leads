## 💰 Key Metrics Explained

### ROI (Return on Investment)
- **Shows**: How much profit you make per dollar spent
- **Formula**: (Revenue - Cost) / Cost × 100
- **Example**: Spend $1,000 → Get $4,000 revenue → ROI = 300%
- **Good benchmark**: > 100% (get 2x back)
- **Great benchmark**: > 300% (get 4x back)
- **Where to find**: ROI & Analytics tab

### CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- **Shows**: Average cost to acquire one paying customer
- **Formula**: Total marketing spend / New customers
- **Example**: Spend $10,000 → Get 20 customers → CAC = $500
- **Rule of thumb**: CAC should be < LTV / 3
- **Where to find**: ROI & Analytics tab

### LTV (Lifetime Value)
- **Shows**: Total revenue from one customer over their lifetime
- **Formula**: (Revenue/customer × Profit margin × Customer lifespan) / Churn rate
- **Example**: $100/mo × 50% margin × 24 months / 10% churn = $10,800
- **Rule of thumb**: LTV:CAC ratio > 3:1 means sustainable growth
- **Where to find**: ROI & Analytics tab

### Win Rate
- **Shows**: % of deals that actually close
- **Formula**: Closed deals / Total deals × 100
- **Example**: 100 demos → 15 close → 15% win rate
- **Benchmark**: 10-20% for most SaaS/B2B
- **Where to find**: Pipeline tab
